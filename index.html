About

A small studio working at the intersection of careful design and considered engineering — close enough to clients to understand the problem, small enough to take the work personally.

leaStudios is based in Boise, Idaho. We build custom websites and native apps for iOS and macOS, for businesses of every size — pairing technical depth with a designer's eye for the details.

Most of our work comes from referral and lasts longer than a single project. We prefer it that way.
